## üîç How to Use

Using **local-knowledge-search** is straightforward:

### 1. Upload Documents

- Open the application.
- Select the option to upload your documents directly from your computer. You can add PDFs, Word files, or text documents.

### 2. Perform a Search

- Once your documents are uploaded, type your search term into the search bar. 
- The AI will scan your documents and return results based on the meaning of your search, not just exact matches.

### 3. Review the Results

- Click on any result to view the document and the context of your search term. This makes it easy to find the specific information you need.
